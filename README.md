# README

## Dreamspace ☁️
*Dreamspace* is a social media platform without the social aspect, just media. 
This is a passion project of mine (as well as my final project for a coding bootcamp) that I plan on continually working on as I keep advancing my skills and knowledge overtime, and hopefully one day be able to deploy it as a fullstack web and mobile application ready for everyone to use and enjoy. *As of now, this is just the first draft.*

## The Vision 👁️
My vision for *Dreamspace* is to create a media sharing platform for everyone, but more importantly, for aspiring artists who struggle with mental health. I personally enjoy being on social media to get inspiration from different types of people who share their art. Unfortunately, these past few years I've been feeling anxious whenever I get on my socials for a lot of reasons, but mostly, it's very triggering to see everything bad that's happening in the world all at once. I don't believe that it's healthy for our mind to absorb everything from everyone whenever we log on instagram, twitter, or facebook. Although it's important to shed light on world crisis, political issues, harrasment, etc.. it's not healthy to take it all in daily and get desensitized by all the horrors of our society, no escape from it unless you log off. I wanted a 'safe' place away from all the unpleasantness and continue to get inspired and immerse myself in art, which is what drives me as a person. That's how I came up with *Dreamspace*, hence the name stating literally *my dream space.*

## As a User, I can... 👤
* Create a free account or delete my account
* Login/Logout
* Create a new post (Blog, Media, Status)
* Archive, Delete, and Edit my post
* Visit other user's profile
* Share other user's post outside of Dreamspace 
* Block a user from seeing my posts and profile
* Follow a user *(if I have their invite link)*
* Share my invite link to be followed by a user *(or personal friend!)*

## Features & Future Features 💡
* **Invite only follows -** each user will have their own shareable profile link and that'll be the only way a user can follow another user.
* **No likes** - users cannot *like* a post, this way an aspiring artist can focus on their work and feel proud as soon as they share it to Dreamspace without that constant need to constantly check how many likes it got and that need to compare yourself and your work from other artists work. 
* **Private comments** - all users can leave a comment on another user's post, but only the owner of that post will see the comments. This method hopefully will make comments more meaningful and heartfelt because they aren't shared to the whole community. 
* **Private notes** - if both users are following each other, both of them can leave little notes on each other's profile. This is meant to be used as a "sticky note" to leave a quick *"Happy Birthday!"* or *"Can I have your email address?"* not a chatroom with all your message threads.  
* **Bookmark posts** - instead of giving a post a like, a user can *bookmark* a post which will save the post under the user's *Bookmarks*, which will be public to the whole community so other users can see what type of media a specific user is interested in.
* **Avatar pictures** - users will not be able to post their own profile picture and will be given a whole range of avatars to choose from instead. The idea behind this is to really focus on a user's work and not on their appearance.
* **No censorship** - users have to be 18 years of age and up because Dreamspace will not censor any images. This way, for example if a user is a photographer, they'll be able to post their work without censorship and then getting shadow banned (like Instagram). Dreamspace is a platfrom for artists, more than anything else. 
* **Statuses** - each user's profile will have a status bar that says **"Currently (reading, watching, listening) to ________"**. The idea is, a user will have set action words as a dropdown to choose from, then be able to provide whicever type of media they're currently enjoying, and be able to save it under their *Statuses*. I personally love looking back and seeing what I was into or doing at a specific time in my life, and this feature is a reflection of that. Each user's statuses will be public, in hopes that if another user visited one user's profile, they can see what this user has read in the past month and get inspired or interested in the same books.
* **Spotify playlist** - I was a huge fan of having music on your social media profile, so I wanted to bring it back. Whenever a user visits another profile, they'll be able to see this user's playlist and get inspiration or honestly just for fun!
* **Monthly Appreciation** - Dreamspace is for everyone, but most importantly, it is for aspiring artists *(writer, designer, singer, etc.)* to truly feel free and work as experimental as they want to be and I would love to highlight their work to show appreciation and help an artist find their voice in the art world. I personally know how a little showcase of your work can reach thousands of other people that could potentially be your future employer, mentor, or who knows what else, the possibilties are endless. I would love for Dreamspace to be able to do that for its community, because *the core vision for this platform is to inspire and celebrate aspiring artists.*
